About

Calvin is a technology professional with knowledge and experience in business intelligence (datawarehouse and analytics), as well as front-end software developement. Instead, he has had the great fortune of working for some of the coolest companies this past 20 years. From being a network manager at the University of Botswana, to IBM Cognos (just Cognos back then), where he worked as a BI Consultant, implementing BI/Datawarehouse solutions for a myriad of clients. And finally, spending close to ten years with Amazon, building and growing the financial planning and analysis platform.
